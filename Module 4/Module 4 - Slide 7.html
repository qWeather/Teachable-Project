Stakeholder Identification

Who are the project stakeholders, both supporters and opponents? Identify them and log the list into a table called STAKEHOLDER MAP

Stakeholder Assessment/Analysis

What is the position, power and influence of each individual stakeholder or stakeholder group? Identify advocates & potential adversities.

Develop Action Plan & Communication Management Plan

Determine methods of communication based on influence, interest, power Determine communication methods with those of different mindsets (positive, negative). Plan conflict resolution strategies

Engage and influence stakeholders

Carry out and maintain the action plans Gain feedback, seek to refine approach for the benefit of the project
