Identify the prime stakeholders and establish a clear communication process to keep the team and all members informed. Determine the channel and frequency of communication

Break down the project into manageable tasks and allocate to appropriate resource. Create a task and time schedule as accurate as possible and include dependencies, milestones and critical paths

Identify all possible risks and establish strategies to prevent them or mitigate their impact

Estimate project costs and create a budget based on these estimations. Regularly compare estimated costs with the real costs to avoid budget overrun

The Planning Phase typically involves:

  • Determining resource availability and allocation
  • Creating a project budget
  • Beginning to allocate tasks to certain resources
