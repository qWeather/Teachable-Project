Present a formal project review report to the client and get their acceptance or “sign-off” on the deliverables presented Ensure that the client is satisfied with the end product. Not down any improvements specified by the client

After completion of every project, it is critical to conduct an analysis on “lessons learned” Generate feedback from client, team members. Assess what went well and what needs improvement. Use this empirical information in improving effectiveness of future projects

The Closure Phase typically involves:

  • Handover to intended users
  • Complete and store documentation
  • Post Implementation Reviews
  • Disbandment of the project team
  • Personnel and tools are reassigned to new duties
