Project Management is the application of processes methods, knowledge, skills and experience to achieve the project objectives.

The project is responsible for day-to-day management of the project and must be competent in managing the six aspects of a project.

  • Scope
  • Schedule
  • Finance
  • Risk
  • Quality
  • Resources

Well-developed interpersonal skills such as leadership, communication and conflict management are also vitally important. The project sponsor is accountable for achievement of the business case and providing senior-level commitment to the project.
