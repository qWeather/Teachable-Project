• A Project Management Methodology is a framework of processes for initiating, planning & managing the Project Life cycle Journey of a Project, using a standard set of tools, techniques, templates & processes, with the right levels of Governance to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget and meet the objectives & quality parameters set.
  • Methodologies are chosen based on scale of projects, significance, availability & clarity of requirements, speed to market, complexity & organization culture.
  • Organisations, sometimes, have standard methodologies that need to be followed by all or certain types of project, and have well defined templates, processes & governance mechanisms that the projects need to follow.

Standard off the shelf methodologies have their own processes defined, inputs & outputs to phases, activities within the phase, review cycles, templates, roles & responsibilities & guidelines for adapting the methodology to different situations.
