It is the environment in which the project is undertaken and is under many influences, generally grouped as external and internal to the project.

  • Sector: Private, Public
  • Industry: FMCG, Finance, Construction, Manufacturing
  • Commercial Nature of Work: Non-profit, Commercial
  • Geographic Location: International, Local, National, Multiple Geographies
  • Scale: Multiple vs. Single Organization
  • Language Differences
  • Regulated Enviroments, Conformance to Standards
  • Insource vs. Outsource vs. Multi Source
  • Single Department vs. Multiple Departments & Functions
  • Political & Social Influences

Broad organization factors outside the project boundaries that still have a significant impact on the way work is approached.
