A Project is a temporary endeavour undertaken to create a unique product, service, process or result.

Building a new School

Developing a new software

Arranging an unique new event in the company

Series of activities
To achieve an output
Via a planned set of deliverables
Within a Measurable time frame
To create something new or improved
At a planned budget (dependent on organization structures)
To meet an organization's strategic need, or a programme's need or a department's need
Intended to bring about change
