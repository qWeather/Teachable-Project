It refers to the project or project team's attributes which helps in achieving business objectives.

Strengths
  • What are the unique skills?
  • What is the expert or specialized knowledge in the team?
  • What experience does the team have?
  • How are your skills better than your competitors?
  • What advantages does the organization have?
  • What unique or lowest-cost resources can be sourced that others can't?
  • What is organization's Unique Selling Proposition (USP)?

Refers to internal team or organization's characteristics that could have a negative impact on business and that could hurt its progress in achieving the desired objectives. Consider factors from an internal and external perspective.

Weaknesses
  • What could be improved?
  • What should be avoided?
  • What competitors or clients perceive as weaknesses?
  • What factors cause failures in project?

These involve external conditions that help in achieving target goals & objectives. What good opportunities can be spotted? What favorable trends coming up?

Opportunities
  • Techonogical and market changes, both on a broad and narrow scale
  • Changes in related field's government policy
  • Changes in social patterns, population profiles, lifestyle changes, and so on

These involve external factors that could damage project's or business' performance.

Threats
  • What obstacles are being face?
  • What are the competitors doing?
  • Any changes in quality standards or specifications related to project, products or services?
  • Threat from change in technology
  • Are there any bad debt or cash-flow problems?
  • Could any of the weaknesses act as serious threat to business?
