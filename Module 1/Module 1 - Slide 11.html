The P3 sponsor or manager needs to perform an assessment of the enviroment as early as possible in the life cycle. Of the typical checklists that exist to help such an assessment, the most common is PESTLE, which stands for political, economic, sociological, technical, legal and ecological factors.

Purpose





  • To identify all the various external enviromental factors that might imapct project or business
  • Helps managers to assess the risks from the identified factors and make informed decisions
  • Reveals all about the circumstances that a project, or organization finds itself in, which helps in making decisions and understanding its current (or future) position in the market
